💡 Product Concept: Contextual Reaction Engine

A modular AI agent system that dynamically reacts to human input — text, voice, or behavior — with personalized reasoning, learning support, or decision-making.

Key Capabilities:

  • 🧠 Persona-Aware Reasoning: Inject voices (teacher, critic, investor) based on article context.
  • 📚 Evolving Knowledge Assistant: Tracks knowledge gaps and adapts (etymology, summaries, testing).
  • 🧩 Function-Triggering via Prompts: "What does this mean?" → word explainer. "Summarize?" → summarizer.
  • 🛠️ Hybrid Memory: MongoDB for real-time memory, SQLite + vector DB for depth and recall.

🚀 Why This Matters

  • 🧑‍🎓 For learners: Like having 3 tutors and a debate coach who remember everything you read.
  • 👨‍💼 For professionals: A thought partner that sharpens insight and adapts to your language.
  • 🧑‍💻 For developers: A programmable AI layer with prompt logic and pluggable tools.
